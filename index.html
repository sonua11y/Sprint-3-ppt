Key Metrics

Impact Score

Composite rating (1–10) based on outcome quality, reach depth, and sustainability of program results

Efficiency = Impact Score ÷ Funds

Measures impact delivered per dollar spent — the true ROI metric for program comparison

Cost per Beneficiary

Total funds ÷ number of beneficiaries — helps assess scale efficiency
